# Driver Drowsiness Detection System - Presentation Guide

**Hello everyone! Today we are presenting our Advanced Driver Drowsiness Detection System.**

---

## 1. Introduction & The Problem
**What is it?**
We have built an AI-powered system designed to monitor a driver in real-time and detect if they are falling asleep, getting distracted manually (like looking at a phone), or exhibiting stress/anger.

**Why is it useful?**
- **Safety First:** Driver fatigue is a leading cause of severe road accidents. Our system acts as an active safety mechanism.
- **Accident Prevention:** By alerting the driver *before* they fall completely asleep or get too distracted, we can save lives and reduce collisions.
- **Data-Driven Logging:** Fleet managers can track driver behavior metrics and long-term fatigue logs.

---

## 2. The Technologies We Used & Why We Chose Them

### 💻 **1. Computer Vision (OpenCV & MediaPipe)**
- **What it does:** Captures real-time video from the camera and maps the driver's face.
- **Why it's useful:** MediaPipe provides extremely fast, precise, and lightweight 468-point 3D face landmarks using minimal CPU resources, meaning our system can run smoothly on a basic laptop or in-car dashboard without requiring an expensive GPU.

### 🧠 **2. Machine Learning Pipeline (XGBoost & SMOTE)**
- **What it does:** Analyzes the facial metrics to decide if the driver is "Normal" or "Sleepy/Distracted".
- **Why it's useful:**
  - **XGBoost** is an incredibly fast, state-of-the-art decision-tree based machine learning model. It provides ~95%+ accuracy for tabular data classification.
  - **SMOTE** (Synthetic Minority Over-sampling Technique) was used to solve "imbalanced data" issues (because we had way more "normal" data than "drowsy" data during training). It helped the AI learn both states equally well.

### 📊 **3. Web Dashboard Engine (Streamlit)**
- **What it does:** Displays an interactive dashboard with real-time analytics.
- **Why it's useful:** It beautifully visualizes our system's live logs, showing pie charts of "Distraction vs. Sleep" and real-time fatigue trend graphs using Plotly, making the system professional and easy to understand for managers.

### 💾 **4. Database Storage (SQLite)**
- **What it does:** Records every single "alert" generated by the camera.
- **Why it's useful:** It allows us to view historical logs. For instance, if an alert happens in our camera application (`advanced_drowsiness_test.py`), it is instantly saved into a relational database and pulled natively by our Dashboard.

## 3. How It Actually Works: The Features

Instead of just looking at the "eyes", our model calculates mathematical ratios based on the physical distance between specific facial points:

1. **EAR (Eye Aspect Ratio):** Calculates how wide open the eyes are. It drops close to zero when the driver blinks or closes their eyes for too long (micro-sleeps).
2. **MAR (Mouth Aspect Ratio):** Calculates how wide the mouth is open. A high MAR indicates the driver is yawning (fatigue) or shouting (quarreling).
3. **MOE (Mouth Over Eye Ratio):** A combined metric that correlates the state of the mouth and eyes, giving a highly accurate indicator of overall exhaustion.
4. **Head Tilt (Pitch/Yaw/Roll):** Checks if the head is dropping down (nodding off) or looking away from the road (distraction).
